COVID-19 can lead to severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) infection, which can lead to severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) -associated coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19), which is characterized by fever, cough, shortness of breath, and acute respiratory distress syndrome (ARDS), with a mortality rate of up to 30% in patients with SARS-CoV-2 infection and a mortality rate of up to 50% in patients with COVID-19.
